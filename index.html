About Us

Women Unified is a long term initiative to chart out sustainable solutions to women's cause. The project aims at bringing about a practical solution to the issues of harassment, exploitation and crimes against women in multiple domains.
The vision is to bring forth a change in the minds of people starting from an individual level and empower women to fight against gender-based violence or abuse. We intend to combine different fields of knowledge and workforce (literature, peace, environment, religion, science, social science, and technology) towards peaceful living.

The Campaign

A social initiative by GLOBAL PEACE FOUNDATION INDIA
A dream of creating mentally and socially enlightened peaceful nation.

Phase I: Identifying the problems.

In the first phase of campaign our team of Coordinators and interns will mainly focus on learning in depth about the social issues, stigmas and taboos that women experience in their day to day lives in the 21st century and how things have changed from decades till now.

Phase II: Analysing the origins.

Here we will analyse the problem statements and try to get to the origins of the major concerns, is there a common pattern around them or they all are mutually exclusive impacts of various causes around the societies and communities these days.

Phase III: Ideation and Brainstorming.

The need of the hour, we'll provide an open platform to the youth for building prototyoes and ideating the possible solutions which will create an impact worth bringing a long term revolution to uplift the status of women in society and they can feel true freedom and empowerment.

Phase IV: Deploying the solution statements.

The final phase is the begining of a nation-wide revolution aimed to successfully implement and expand the solutions and strategies devised from the hardship and fruitful continuous efforts of the Women Unified Community and this legacy should pass on to our future generations
